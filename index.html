Post-Uni (2022-Today)

Following my graduation from University, I embarked on a freeCodeCamp course focusing on Responsive Web Design, an experience that I thoroughly enjoyed and which ultimately led me to create my own portfolio website. Concurrently, I secured my first job post University as an Assistant Installation Engineer at Absolute Digital Solutions designing and installing CCTV security systems. This position was initially intended as a short-term engagement lasting six months, however I ended up staying for 10 months following our teams good work and to be honest because I felt very comfortable in my job role. I enjoyed my time in the industry but I knew that in the long term my career goals were aimed at becoming a software developer and so I parted ways with my first job in hope of more opportunities in the software development world. Now after numerous applications in the job market yielding no success, I feel like maybe Im not ready to go into a entry-level job in the software industry, then I was fortunate enough to receive a recommendation for FAC and I learned about the program and discovered that it sounds like a great opportunity for where I am at in my programming and career journey.

Why do I want to join Founders and Coders?

I am drawn to the prospect of transitioning into a career as a web developer for several compelling reasons. First and foremost, the dynamic and ever-evolving nature of web development excites me, as it offers continuous opportunities for learning and growth. The ability to create and shape digital experiences, coupled with the tangible impact on users worldwide, is a powerful motivator. Moreover, the collaborative nature of web development fosters a sense of community and teamwork that I find appealing. The prospect of bringing creative ideas to life through coding and problem-solving appeals to my intrinsic curiosity and passion for technology. In a world increasingly reliant on digital platforms, becoming a web developer not only aligns with my interests but also ensures that I contribute to the innovative landscape of the internet, making a meaningful and lasting impact on the way people interact with information and technology.
